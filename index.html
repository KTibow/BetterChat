BetterChat

The best chat filter for Hypixel.

BetterChat is a ChatTriggers mod to improve your Hypixel chatting experience.

BetterChat is a mod that uses Bayesian analysis to perform lightning-fast and accurate classification to hide spam. It hides people begging, advertising, etc.

It also has a couple other miscellaneous features, such as hiding ranks to remove clutter, and it's still an actively-developed mod.
